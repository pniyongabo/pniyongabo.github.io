2024 - Wrapped

I have been meaning to write this for the last 5 years or so. But I always ended up being preoccupied with something else. Work, school, travel, life ups and downs, you name it. All that to say that this is the first holiday season where I have enough free time to post something.

2024 was an eventful year. And so was 2023, and the pandemic era that preceded it. A lot has happened in my personal life. Similarly a lot has happened in the world, especially in the tech industry where I make a living.

At first, I thought I would write this post as a collection of learnings from my personal experiences, but that felt like too much pressure after such an extended hiatus. Instead I am keeping it light with a “Spotify Wrapped” style overview of my 2024 highlights and 2025 goals.
