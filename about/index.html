A Bit About Me

I am an AI & ML Graduate Student at Portland State University. For college, I attended Hendrix College as a Presidential Scholar, and I studied Computer Science (Major) and international business (minor). I also spent the Spring Semester of 2016 studying abroad in England at Nottingham Trent University.

Before college, I attended Petit Séminaire Saint Jean Nkumba for 6 years. That is 3 years of middle school plus 3 years of high school spent at the same school! Go Imparirwakurusha!

In my free time, I enjoy traveling, reading, blogging & tweeting, coding & debugging, playing Fifa & Fútbol, et cetera! I like to think that the list of my hobbies is a never-ending one!
